Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI Code Judge.

How does your AI analyze code?

Our advanced AI doesn't actually analyze your code at all. It just runs a random number generator to determine how harshly to criticize you, then selects pre-written insults from a database. The technology is indistinguishable from magic!

Is my code secure on your platform?

Absolutely! We don't store your code because, frankly, we don't want to. Our servers would crash from the sheer horror of what most people submit. However, we do reserve the right to use exceptionally bad code examples for internal comedy nights.

Will your AI help me improve my coding skills?

Studies show that crushing someone's self-esteem is actually a terrible way to help them improve. However, our unnecessarily harsh feedback might motivate you to prove us wrong, which is a valid (if emotionally damaging) learning strategy.
